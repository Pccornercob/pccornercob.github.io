Laptop Spare Parts

At PC Corner, we provide a comprehensive range of laptop spare parts to keep your devices running smoothly and efficiently. Whether you need replacements for damaged components, upgrades to enhance performance, or accessories to improve usability, we supply high-quality, genuine parts for all major laptop brands, including HP, Dell, Lenovo, Acer, Asus, Toshiba, Apple, and many more. Our goal is to offer reliable, cost-effective solutions that ensure your laptop performs at its best—whether for home, office, or professional use.

Wide Range of Laptop Spare Parts Available:

We stock and supply an extensive inventory of laptop components, ensuring that every replacement or upgrade meets strict quality standards. Our range includes:

Motherboards: Original and compatible motherboards for all major brands, tested and certified for performance and durability.

Keyboards: Full and partial keyboard replacements, including backlit, multilingual, and water-resistant models.

Screens & Displays: High-definition LED, LCD, and touch-screen panels in various sizes and resolutions.

Batteries: Genuine and long-lasting lithium-ion batteries offering maximum backup and safety.

Chargers & Adapters: Original and compatible power adapters designed for safe, efficient charging.

RAM (Memory): DDR2, DDR3, DDR4, and DDR5 memory modules for smooth multitasking and enhanced system performance.

Hard Drives & SSDs: High-speed storage options, including HDDs, SATA SSDs, and NVMe drives for faster data transfer.

Cooling Fans & Heat Sinks: Replacement fans, cooling pads, and thermal solutions to prevent overheating.

Cables & Connectors: DC jacks, power cables, ribbon cables, and internal connectors for all laptop models.

Speakers, Webcams & Microphones: High-quality replacements for multimedia and communication components.

Hinges, Frames & Casings: Durable structural parts to restore the look and stability of your laptop.

Wi-Fi Cards & Network Modules: For enhanced wireless connectivity and faster data transmission.
