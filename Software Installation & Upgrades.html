Software Installation & Upgrades

At PC Corner, we provide expert Software Installation and Upgrade Services designed to keep your systems running efficiently, securely, and up to date with the latest technology. In today’s fast-paced digital world, having the right software—and ensuring it’s properly installed and maintained—is essential for productivity, performance, and protection.

Whether you’re setting up a brand-new computer or updating older systems to meet modern standards, our team of skilled technicians handles everything from software selection to installation, configuration, licensing, and optimization. We ensure that your operating system, applications, and drivers work seamlessly together, giving you a fast, secure, and reliable computing experience.

Comprehensive Software Solutions

Our Software Installation & Upgrade services cover a wide range of needs for individuals, offices, and enterprises alike.

Operating System Installation & Configuration

We install and configure all major operating systems, including:

  • Windows (7, 8, 10, 11, Server Editions).
  • macOS and Linux Distributions.
  • Custom OS environments for specific business applications.

Our experts ensure correct partitioning, formatting, driver installation, and activation to deliver a fully functional, optimized operating system tailored to your usage needs.

Application and Productivity Software

We install, configure, and update essential productivity software, including:

  • Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).
  • Google Workspace, LibreOffice, or OpenOffice.
  • Accounting software (Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho Books).
  • Design and creative tools (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, CorelDRAW).
  • Development tools (Visual Studio, Eclipse, JetBrains IDEs).
  • Communication software (Zoom, Teams, Skype, Slack).

Each installation is customized to suit your workflow, with shortcuts, permissions, and preferences set up for maximum convenience.

Driver and Firmware Updates: Outdated drivers can lead to hardware malfunctions, crashes, or compatibility issues. We update and configure all system drivers—graphics, audio, network, chipset, and printer drivers—ensuring your hardware runs at its best performance.

Security and Antivirus Software: Security is a top priority. We install and configure the latest antivirus, anti-malware, and firewall solutions to protect your data and system from cyber threats. Our technicians also perform periodic security updates and patch installations to keep your defenses strong.

Business and Enterprise Software Installation

For businesses, we install and configure enterprise-grade software, including:

  • ERP and CRM solutions.
  • Database management systems (MySQL, SQL Server, Oracle).
  • Virtualization and cloud tools (VMware, Hyper-V, Azure, AWS).
  • Server software and remote access tools.
  • Collaboration and project management platforms.

We ensure that every system in your network is properly licensed, synchronized, and connected.

Software Upgrades and Patch Management: Technology evolves rapidly, and keeping your software up to date is crucial for stability and security. We handle version upgrades, feature updates, and patches for your existing applications—without disrupting your work or data.

Troubleshooting and Reinstallation: If your current software is malfunctioning, crashing, or running slowly, we diagnose the problem and reinstall or repair it. We also recover lost configurations, restore corrupted programs, and resolve compatibility issues.

Data Backup Before Installation: Before any major software installation or upgrade, we perform a complete data backup to prevent data loss. Your important files, settings, and preferences are safely preserved and restored after setup.

Our Software Installation Process

At PC Corner, we follow a systematic, transparent process to ensure safe and efficient software installation and upgrades:

Requirement Analysis – We understand your needs, usage patterns, and software requirements.

Compatibility Check – We verify system specifications and hardware compatibility before installation.

Software Selection and Licensing – We assist in choosing the right software editions (Standard, Professional, Enterprise) and ensure genuine, licensed versions.

Installation and Configuration – Our technicians handle complete setup, configuration, and activation.

Testing and Optimization – We test the installed software for stability, performance, and compatibility with other programs.

User Training and Handover – We provide basic guidance on software usage, shortcuts, and updates.

Ongoing Support – Post-installation support for troubleshooting, maintenance, and upgrades
