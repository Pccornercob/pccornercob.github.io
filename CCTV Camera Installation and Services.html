CCTV Camera Installation and Services

At PC Corner, we specialize in providing comprehensive CCTV camera installation and maintenance services designed to enhance safety, protect property, and ensure peace of mind. In today’s world, security is not optional—it’s essential. Whether it’s for your home, office, business, or industrial premises, our team delivers professional, reliable, and customized surveillance solutions using the latest technologies and trusted brands.

Our CCTV systems are tailored to meet your exact needs—whether you require a simple setup for basic monitoring or a complex, multi-location surveillance system with advanced features like remote viewing, motion detection, and cloud storage.

Complete CCTV Installation Solutions

We provide end-to-end CCTV services, covering every stage of setup and operation:

  • Site Survey and Consultation: Our experts conduct an on-site inspection to assess your property, identify vulnerable areas, and determine the best camera placement for maximum coverage and efficiency.
  • System Design and Planning: Based on your security requirements, we design a customized CCTV system using high-quality cameras and accessories that fit your space, budget, and goals.
  • Professional Installation: Our skilled technicians ensure precise installation of cameras, wiring, DVRs/NVRs, and related components. Every system is neatly installed, tested, and configured for optimal performance and durability.
  • Configuration and Integration: We set up your system for real-time monitoring and remote access via smartphones, tablets, or computers. Our team configures alerts, motion detection zones, and recording schedules to maximize security efficiency.
  • Testing and Quality Check: After installation, we perform a complete system test to verify image quality, connectivity, power stability, and recording accuracy, ensuring everything functions perfectly before handover.
  • Training and Handover: We provide hands-on training to help you operate and manage your CCTV system, including playback, recording access, mobile viewing, and maintenance tips.

Types of CCTV Systems We Install

We offer a wide range of CCTV systems to suit different environments and security needs:

  • Analog CCTV Systems: Cost-effective surveillance ideal for small offices, homes, and retail spaces.
  • HD CCTV Systems: Delivers clear, high-definition video footage for enhanced monitoring.
  • IP CCTV Systems: Advanced digital systems that offer remote access, scalability, and high-resolution recording.
  • Wireless CCTV Systems: Easy-to-install systems with minimal wiring and flexible placement options.
  • PTZ (Pan-Tilt-Zoom) Cameras: For wide coverage, zoom functionality, and real-time control of viewing angles.
  • Infrared/Night Vision Cameras: Ensures visibility and protection even in low-light or complete darkness.
