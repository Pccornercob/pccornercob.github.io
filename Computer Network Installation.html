Our Network Installation Process

At PC Corner, we follow a systematic approach to ensure every network installation is efficient, secure, and future-ready:

Consultation and Site Assessment: We begin by understanding your organization’s requirements—how many users, what type of devices, data transfer needs, and internet bandwidth usage. Then we conduct a thorough site survey to analyze the building layout, signal reach, and cabling requirements.

Network Design and Planning: Based on the assessment, we design a custom network layout that ensures optimal coverage, minimal interference, and maximum efficiency. Our team selects the best combination of wired and wireless infrastructure to suit your needs.

Hardware and Component Selection: We provide and install high-quality, branded networking components such as routers, switches, modems, patch panels, and cabling (Cat6, Cat6A, or fiber optic). We ensure every device is compatible, durable, and capable of supporting future upgrades.

Installation and Configuration: Our skilled technicians handle the complete installation—from cabling and mounting access points to configuring routers, switches, and firewalls. We ensure clean wiring, professional finishing, and adherence to safety standards.

Network Testing and Optimization: After installation, we conduct detailed testing for signal strength, speed, latency, and stability. We fine-tune network performance and eliminate bottlenecks to ensure smooth data transmission across all connected devices.

Security Implementation: Security is a top priority. We configure firewalls, encryption protocols, and password policies to protect your network from unauthorized access, malware, and cyber threats.

Documentation and Training: Once installation is complete, we provide detailed network documentation (including layout diagrams and login credentials) and train your staff on basic network operations and maintenance.

Ongoing Support and Maintenance: We offer continued support through maintenance contracts, troubleshooting visits, and performance checks to keep your network secure and efficient long after installation.
