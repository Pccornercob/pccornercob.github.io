Custom-Built Computers for Every Purpose

We understand that every user has different computing demands. That’s why we design and assemble systems specifically tailored to each purpose:

  • Gaming PCs: For gamers seeking ultra-smooth performance, high frame rates, and stunning graphics, we build powerful rigs featuring the latest CPUs, GPUs, cooling systems, and RGB aesthetics. Each build is optimized for popular titles and future-proofed for upcoming games.
  • Professional Workstations: Perfect for designers, video editors, architects, programmers, and engineers who require powerful computing capabilities. Our workstations are optimized for tasks like 3D rendering, CAD applications, software development, and video production.
  • Office & Business Systems: Reliable, energy-efficient, and cost-effective systems for everyday business tasks, data processing, and multitasking. Ideal for offices, educational institutions, and administrative environments.
  • Home & Student PCs: Affordable, efficient, and compact builds for general use, online learning, entertainment, and multimedia consumption.
  • Servers & Network Systems: High-performance builds tailored for enterprise environments, small businesses, and home networks—ensuring stable operation and scalable performance.

Our Computer Assembly Process

At PC Corner, every system is carefully assembled, tested, and optimized for peak performance. Our process includes:

Consultation and Requirement Analysis: We start by understanding your computing needs, preferences, and budget. Based on your requirements, we recommend suitable components and configurations.

Component Selection: We help you choose from top-quality components including processors (Intel, AMD), motherboards, RAM, graphics cards, storage drives (SSD/HDD), power supplies, cooling systems, and cases. Every part is selected for performance, reliability, and compatibility.

Precision Assembly: Our expert technicians handle the physical assembly with utmost care, ensuring clean cabling, proper airflow, and optimal layout for cooling and aesthetics.

Software Installation & Optimization: We install operating systems, essential drivers, and requested software. The system is optimized for stability, security, and speed right out of the box.

Testing & Quality Assurance: Every custom-built PC undergoes rigorous testing for performance, temperature control, and system stability. Stress tests ensure that your system performs flawlessly under heavy workloads or gaming conditions.

Delivery & Support: Once fully tested, your custom PC is delivered ready to use. We also provide after-sales support, guidance on upgrades, and maintenance services to ensure long-term satisfaction.
