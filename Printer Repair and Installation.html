Printer Repair and Installation

Printers are essential tools in every home, office, and business. From printing reports and invoices to photos and marketing materials, they keep your work flowing efficiently. However, like any other device, printers can develop technical issues due to wear, connectivity problems, or software errors.

At PC Corner, we specialize in comprehensive Printer Repair and Installation Services for all types and brands of printers. Whether you’re facing paper jams, faded prints, connection issues, or complete printer failure, our experienced technicians provide fast, professional, and cost-effective solutions to get your printer back in perfect working condition

We also handle new printer installation, setup, and configuration — ensuring that your printer is properly connected, optimized, and ready to deliver high-quality output from day one.

Complete Printer Repair & Installation Services

Our expert team offers end-to-end services that cover every aspect of printer repair, servicing, and setup:

Printer Diagnosis and Troubleshooting: We begin with a detailed diagnosis of your printer to identify the root cause of the issue. Using advanced tools and years of experience, we can detect both hardware and software-related problems such as:

  • Paper feed and jam issues.
  • Blurred, faded, or uneven print quality.
  • Cartridge recognition errors.
  • Printer not responding or showing offline.
  • Power supply and connectivity faults.
  • Firmware and driver problems.
  • Strange noises or mechanical failures.

Our technicians provide accurate assessments and suggest the most cost-effective repair options for your specific printer model.

Printer Hardware Repairs and Replacement: Hardware issues are among the most common causes of printer malfunctions. We handle all major hardware repairs, including:

  • Replacement of rollers, gears, and belts.
  • Repair of fuser units, heating elements, and print heads.
  • Power supply repair and replacement.
  • Paper tray and cassette fixes.
  • Optical sensor and board-level repairs.
  • Cartridge and toner mechanism servicing.

We use genuine OEM and compatible spare parts to ensure durability, performance, and long-term reliability.

Printer Software and Driver Issues: Many printer issues are software-related, especially in networked or multi-user environments. We can resolve problems such as:

  • Missing or corrupt printer drivers.
  • Installation errors or compatibility issues.
  • Printing queue and spooler problems.
  • Network printing errors.
  • Firmware updates and configuration resets.

We install the latest printer drivers and firmware to ensure your device communicates seamlessly with your computer or network.

Printer Installation and Setup: Bought a new printer? Let PC Corner handle the entire installation process! Our installation service includes:

  • Unboxing and initial setup.
  • Connecting printers to computers, laptops, or mobile devices.
  • Configuring USB, Wi-Fi, and network printers.
  • Installing printer drivers and software.
  • Setting up default preferences and print quality settings.
  • Testing functionality (print, scan, copy, fax).
  • Configuring multi-user access for offices or workgroups.

We make sure your printer is fully operational and integrated with your existing systems before we leave.

Network Printer Configuration: For offices and organizations, network printing is essential for efficiency. We specialize in:

  • Setting up shared printers on LAN and Wi-Fi networks.
  • Configuring printer servers and remote printing access.
  • Assigning IP addresses and ensuring stable connectivity.
  • Troubleshooting printer discovery and permission issues.
  • Setting up secure printing and access control.

Our experts ensure smooth, secure, and reliable network printing across all your workstations.

Printer Maintenance & Servicing: Regular maintenance prevents costly breakdowns and keeps your printer in top condition. Our maintenance services include:

  • Cleaning of rollers, print heads, and sensors.
  • Lubrication and mechanical tuning.
  • Alignment calibration and test printing.
  • Toner and ink level checks.
  • Firmware and software updates.
  • Replacing worn-out components before failure.

We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) for homes, offices, schools, and enterprises that require continuous service and part support.

Printer Calibration and Optimization: If your printer is producing inconsistent prints, we perform detailed calibration to restore perfect accuracy. This includes:

  • Print head alignment.
  • Color calibration and tone balance.
  • Resolution and print quality optimization.
  • Media type and size configuration.

Whether you print documents, labels, or photos, we make sure every output is crisp, clear, and professional.

Types of Printers We Service and Install: Our services cover all types of printers, regardless of brand or complexity:

  • Inkjet Printers – Common for homes and small offices.
  • Laser Printers – Ideal for heavy-duty printing in offices.
  • Dot Matrix Printers – Used for billing and multi-copy printing.
  • Multifunction Printers (MFPs) – Print, scan, copy, and fax machines.
  • Photo and Large Format Printers – For photographers and graphic designers.
  • Thermal Printers – Used for barcode, billing, and POS systems.
